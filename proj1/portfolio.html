Here's some of my past work:
SpokeIt
SpokeIt is a cleft speech therapy mobile game I helped create at an internship at the UC Santa Cruz Baskin School of Engineering. I primarily used Swift and XCode to program device compatibility and enhanced speech recognition features.

Later, I was put in charge of writing code documentation for earning the game a patent. The app is currently still under development, and the main website can be found at spokeitthegame.com
Research Paper
I coauthored a research paper that advocates for universal design and provides concepts and themes to inspire future technology. I collected and analyzed hundreds of TikTok vidoes in order to categorize them into seven emerging themes. I then used these themes to generate my own technological designs.
This paper scored in the top 3% of 2844 paper submissions for the ACM SIGCHI Digital Library before being presented at the SIGCHI 2021 International Conference in Yokohama, Japan. The link to the paper can be found here.
